Privacy Policy for DocHub - Calendar & Appointments

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

Introduction

DocHub ("we", "us", or "our") operates the DocHub mobile application (the "App"), a calendar and appointment management platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for scheduling and managing appointments.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and Contact Information: Full name, phone number, and email address
  • Appointment Information: Appointment details, scheduling preferences, and appointment notes
  • Authentication Data: Account credentials for doctor login (encrypted)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: App features accessed, time spent, and interaction patterns
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance
  • Calendar Integration Data: Calendar events and scheduling information (when you grant permission)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  1. Appointment Management: Scheduling appointments, managing calendars, and coordinating meetings
  2. Authentication: Secure login for users and service providers
  3. Communication: Sending appointment confirmations and updates
  4. Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance app functionality
  5. Legal Compliance: Meeting data protection regulations and legal requirements

Data Storage and Security

Firebase Cloud Services

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase services, which include:

  • Firestore Database: Encrypted storage for appointments, calendar data, and user preferences
  • Firebase Authentication: Secure user authentication
  • Data Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  1. With Service Providers: Your information is shared with the specific service providers you book appointments with
  2. Third-Party Services: Calendar services (Google Calendar, Outlook) when you enable integration
  3. Service Providers: Third-party services (Firebase, Google) that help us operate the app
  4. Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  5. With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize data sharing

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format

  • User privacy protection
  • Transparent data practices

Data Retention

  • Appointment Records: Retained for 3 years for service quality and dispute resolution
  • Chat Messages: Retained for 1 year

  • Account Deletion: All data permanently deleted within 30 days upon request
